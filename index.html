Title: APAC-AFF URL ADMIN SYSTEM
Finished At: April 2018
Tools Used: PHP, MySQL, ChartJS, Javscript, Jquery
Description:

This system is built to give assitance doing digital marketing. Its main purpose is to track your progress on projects and deals. The functionalities of the system are as follows:

  • Managing of websites (Hostings, Servers, Country and Expirations)
  • Piwik API tracker (gets website data from Piwik's server)
  • Managing of websites articles - with scheduling depending on website type
  • Managing Operators Information
  • Assistance/Follow-UP on your SEO Strategies
  • Managing articles for your websites
  • Accounting system for your article's translators
  • Notification system
